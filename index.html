Open Science Workshops

Welcome

We are launching Open Science Workshops in order to bring open source techniques to the science community.

Goal

Contribute to the betterment of the world by making it easier for people to do science; i.e. make it easy to replicate results, understand results, collaborate, and build upon existing work.

The event

It's completely free, and being held on the 12th of July, 2014, in Melbourne, Australia.

Help with the workshop!

We are currently looking for volunteers to be involved in all aspects of this project! For the upcoming workshop in Melbourne, if you are willing to:

  • talk about GitHub and help with setup,
  • talk about IPython, help with basics,
  • be excited and generally help out,
then we want to hear from you! To get involved, email Noon Silk, or join the mailing list ows-discuss.

Sponsor the workshop!

The workshop is being run entirely for free by a team of volunteers. We aren't interested in profit, but need to pay fees to the venues, and provide morning/afternoon tea for the attendees. If you are an organisation interested in supporting our aims, please contact Noon Silk.

The details of all funding will be completely disclosed, i.e. in the spirit of openness, the following google doc Sponsors/Expenses for OSW Melbourne 2014 will be updated with the relevant details.
