Open Science Workshops

Welcome

We are launching Open Science Workshops in order to bring open source techniques to the science community.

Goal

Our aim is basically contribute generally to the betterment of the world at large by helping scientists share their work.

Our essential motivation is to achieve:

Openness, reproducability, valuable scientific work and insights available to all freely.

Get involved

We are currently looking for volunteers to be involved in all aspects of this project! For the upcoming workshop in Melbourne, if you are willing to:

  • talk about GitHub and help with setup,
  • talk about ipython, help with basics,
  • talk about sage, help with basics,
we want to hear from you! To get involved, email Noon Silk.
